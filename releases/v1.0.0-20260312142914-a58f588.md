# Podhoc v1.0.0-20260312142914-a58f588

- Initial release of Podhoc for base version 1.0.0.

## What’s new

- **New multilingual website**
  - New marketing site with a clearer story about what Podhoc does.
  - Available in many languages so more people can understand the product.
  - Improved search and social previews so shared links look better.

- **More reliable podcast creation**
  - Better handling of long videos, articles, and other content.
  - Fewer failed jobs thanks to improved error handling and automatic retries.
  - Clearer behavior when something goes wrong, so users know what to do next.

- **Simpler pricing and credits**
  - Credits and subscription tiers now behave more consistently across the app.
  - Fixes for edge cases when changing plans (upgrades/downgrades).
  - Behind-the-scenes improvements to how usage is calculated.

- **Smoother signup and login**
  - More robust signup and login flows, including social sign-in.
  - Improved email verification and password reset experience.
  - Fewer “stuck” states when coming back after a long time away.

- **Better support experience**
  - Replies to support emails are now more reliably connected to their original tickets.
  - Makes it easier for the team to follow issues end to end.

- **Security and stability updates**
  - Tighter handling of logs and internal errors to better protect user data.
  - Infrastructure and monitoring improvements to keep Podhoc stable under load.
